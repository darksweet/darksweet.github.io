NEIGHBOUR NOISE SIMULATION KIT

Parts / 部件

Functions / 功能

The instrument (the box) is a miniaturized and simplified structure of a typical room that detects surrounding noise. With the power of this box-sized room, You can be your own loud neighbor from all directions. For example Tapping on the left side of the box’s ceiling, you will hear the neighbor noise from the left through your headphones. Finger-tapping across the ceiling from front to right, you will hear the noise from the corresponding directions, as if someone is heavy-walking upstairs.

It’s a self-contained instrument that projects the spatial sound(Currently in 2D, no Up and Down Variation) within the chamber to a spatial sound system or stereo headphone.
